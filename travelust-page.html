TRAVELUST APP

UX/UI Design

February 2025

Project Vision

Travelust is a travel focused app made for everyone who is passioned with exploring new countries and cities. This app let's you plan your trips, track the countdown for it, share the moments with your friends, relive it whenever you want and track your world exploration progress.

Challenges

a) Make a visual appealing product with a unique design.

b) Create a user friendly product with a simple navigation.

c) Give as many features as possible taking into account the traveller needs

Define the goals

With the help of an affinitty diagram, I started to list some features that would be usefull for a traveller

Meet the Users

Based on the 3 goals created in the past research, I created 2 different User Personas, imagining what type of users would use the app and would be their goals and problems.

After creating the personas, I created two goal statements to clarify the goals that I would need to follow during the process.

Goal Statement 1:

Our travel app will let users make a custom map with interest points which will affect how people plan their trips by giving them the ability to see a countdown page with every future trip

Goal Statement 2:

Our travel app will let users add photos and details of their trips which will affect how people save their trips by giving them access of a special memories section with all the details and photos of each trip

User Journey

With the goals well defined, I made a user journey based on the main task the users could take, taking our quiz planner to get a personalized trip recommendation. All this in order to know what screens should I create in the next steps.
