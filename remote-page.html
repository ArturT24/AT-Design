REMOTE WORK APP

UX/UI Design

October 2023

Project Vision

An app focused on making the remote work experience better, allowing to make video calls and helping to separate the working hours from the free time.

Challenges

a) Create something usable for a remote work environment.

b) Create an efficient, effective and satisfactory product, following the usability rules.

c) Fit the product into the remote work experience.

Define the goals

With the help of an affinitty diagram and some researched data from surveys, I started to write all the insights about people’s opinion about working remotely. After writing them all, I divided them in topics, and with them I was able to get some pros and cons about remote work that helped me guide through the rest of the process.

Meet the Users

Based on the pros and cons, I created a user persona that could fit into that list

After creating the persona, I created one goal statement to clarify the goals that I would need to follow during the process.

Goal Statement:

Our remote work app will let users focus more on a remote work situation which will affect how people feel in that type of situation by helping them to separate their work and daily home routine.

User Journey Map

With the goals well defined, I created a user journey map, to help identify possible pain points and their solution during the steps of a user searching, finding and exploring the product.

Crazy Eight

With the goals well establish, I decided to do a “crazy eight”, which consists in 8 spaces to draw 8 solutions, in 8 minutes (1 minute per space), with the goal of gathering multiple possible solutions.
